## Overview

Notify your PubNub channels about feature flag changes in real time. This integration ensures that any updates in ConfigCat are instantly propagated across your distributed systems via PubNub.

## Installation

<Steps>
<Step>
Have a <a href="https://www.pubnub.com/" target="_blank">PubNub account.</a>
</Step>
<Step>
Get your PubNub publish key, subscribe key from the <a href="https://admin.pubnub.com/" target="_blank">PubNub admin portal.</a> 
<img src="/docs/assets/pubnub/pubnub_keys.png" alt="PubNub keys" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />
</Step>
<Step>
Open the <a href="https://app.configcat.com/product/integrations" target="_blank">integrations tab</a> on ConfigCat Dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click on PubNub's CONNECT button and set your PubNub publish key, subscribe key and set the channel where you want to receive the notifications.
</Step>
<Step>
You're all set. Go ahead and make some changes on your feature flags, then check your messages in PubNub.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Un-installation
<Steps>
<Step>
Open the <a href="https://app.configcat.com/product/integrations" target="_blank">integrations tab</a> on ConfigCat Dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click on PubNub's DISCONNECT button.
</Step>
</Steps>
