Connect ConfigCat with Okta via SAML.

## Introduction

Each SSO Identity Provider requires specific information to configure a SAML integration. The following guide will walk you through how you can connect ConfigCat with Okta as a SAML Identity Provider.

## 1. Create an Application in Okta

- Log in to <a href="https://login.okta.com/" target="_blank">Okta</a>, go to the admin Dashboard, and select `Applications`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/applications.png" alt="Okta applications" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Click on `Create App Integration`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/create_app.png" alt="Okta create app" decoding="async" loading="lazy"/>

- Select `SAML 2.0` as the Sign-in method.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/select_saml.png" alt="Okta select SAML" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Enter a descriptive `App name`, then click `Next`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/app_name.png" alt="Okta app name" decoding="async" loading="lazy"/>

The next step will guide you on how to collect the information required for the appearing `Configure SAML` section.

## 2. Configure SAML for the Okta Application

- Open your organization's authentication settings on the <a href="https://app.configcat.com/organization/authentication" target="_blank">ConfigCat Dashboard</a>.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/dashboard/authentication.png" alt="ConfigCat authentication settings" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Click `ADD SAML IDENTITY PROVIDER`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/dashboard/add_idp.png" alt="ConfigCat Add Identity Provider" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Give a name for your Identity Provider, and click `Create`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/dashboard/okta_name.png" alt="ConfigCat Name Identity Provider" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- From the next section of the dialog, copy the following values and paste them into the Okta application.

  - `Entity ID` -> `Audience URI (SP Entity ID)`
  - `Assertion Consumer Service` -> `Single sign on URL`

    <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/dashboard/acs_entity_id_1.png" alt="ConfigCat SAML configuration" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

    <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/okta_acs_eid.png" alt="Okta SAML url EID" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Set the `Name ID format` to `EmailAddress`, then click `Next`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/okta_name_id.png" alt="Okta SAML nameid" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Select `I'm an Okta customer adding an internal app`. Complete the form with any comments and click `Finish`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/feedback.png" alt="Okta SAML feedback" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

## 4. Assign Users to Okta Application

To let users authenticate via SAML, you need to assign individual users or groups to the Okta application.

- Select the `Assignments` tab, and select either the `Assign to People` or the `Assign to Groups` option.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/assign.png" alt="Okta assign to groups" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

## 5. Sign In

- Go to the <a href="https://app.configcat.com" target="_blank">ConfigCat Log In</a> page, and click `COMPANY ACCOUNT - SAML`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/dashboard/saml_login.png" alt="ConfigCat SAML login" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- Sign in with your company email address assigned to the Okta application.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/dashboard/company_email.png" alt="ConfigCat SAML company login" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- ConfigCat will redirect you to Okta's sign in page. Type your credentials, and click `Sign In`.

  <img className="bordered-img" src="/docs/assets/saml/okta/okta_sign_in.png" alt="Okta sign in" decoding="async" loading="lazy" />

- You should be redirected to ConfigCat signed in with your company account.
